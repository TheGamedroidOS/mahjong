Goal

Remove all tiles from the board by matching pairs of identical tiles.

Rules

Open tiles: A tile is open if it has no tiles above it and at least one side (left or right) is free.

Matching: Two tiles match if they have identical symbols. Flowers and seasons can match within their groups.
